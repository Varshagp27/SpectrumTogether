Spectrum Together

Empowering the Autism & Disability Community

Welcome to Spectrum Together, a place where we unite to support individuals with autism, disabilities, along with their families. Our mission is to create a caring community, offer resources, and provide assistance that promotes inclusion, independence, and emotional well-being.

We are affliated to Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities which works as the governmental level to ensure the rights of people with disabilities are protected..
